Netflix Privacy Statement

This Privacy Statement explains how we collect, use, and disclose your personal information when you interact with the Netflix service or anywhere we display or reference this Privacy Statement.

It also explains what privacy rights you have and how to exercise them. Certain functionalities or apps that are part of the Netflix service may also provide you with contextual privacy information or choices.

Contacting Us

For questions about this Privacy Statement, our use of your personal information, or how to exercise your privacy rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer/Privacy Office at privacy@netflix.com.

For general questions about the Netflix service, your account, or customer support, please visit the Netflix Help Center.

Section A: Our Collection, Use, and Disclosure of Personal Information

The Categories of Personal Information We Collect

We collect various categories of personal information about you, including personal details, payment details, purchase information, account information, usage information, advertising information, device and network information, and communications.

Personal Details

When you register with Netflix, we collect your contact information such as your email address and login credentials. Depending on your interaction with the service, we may also collect your name, phone number, postal address, gender, date of birth, and other identifiers.

Payment Details

We collect payment information including your payment method, billing address, payment history, redeemed gift cards, and promotional offers used on your account.

Purchase Information

We collect information related to purchases made through Netflix, including purchase history, products viewed or considered, and overall purchasing habits.

Netflix Account and Profile Information

We collect information associated with your Netflix account and profiles, including profile names, profile icons, game handles, ratings, feedback, watch lists, viewing activity, account settings, and preferences.

Usage Information

We collect information about how you interact with Netflix, including viewing activity, gameplay activity, search history, voice commands, app interactions, page views, and time spent using the service.

Advertising Information

We collect information about advertisements you view or interact with, device identifiers, IP addresses, household preferences, demographic information, and interests inferred from online activities.

Device and Network Information

We collect information about devices used to access Netflix, including device identifiers, IP addresses, browser information, connection type, performance reports, cookies, and advertising identifiers.

Communications

When you contact Netflix customer support, participate in surveys, or communicate with us, we collect the contents of those communications and information about your interaction with messages we send.

Where We Collect Personal Information From

We collect personal information directly from you, automatically when you use our service, from partners, service providers, advertising companies, marketing providers, public sources, and third-party platforms.

How We Use Your Personal Information

We use personal information to provide and improve Netflix services, process payments, personalize recommendations, conduct research, send communications, support advertisements, prevent fraud, maintain security, and comply with legal obligations.

Who We Disclose Personal Information To

We may disclose personal information to Netflix companies, service providers, partners, advertising companies, marketing providers, promotional collaborators, and legal authorities when required by law.

International Transfers of Personal Information

Netflix operates globally and may transfer your personal information to countries with different data protection laws. We use contractual, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect personal information during these transfers.

Section B: Your Rights and Controls

Your Privacy Rights

You may have the right to access, correct, update, delete, download, or transfer your personal information. You may also object to processing, restrict processing, withdraw consent, and file complaints with data protection authorities.

Communication and Marketing Preferences

You can manage email, text message, push notification, and marketing preferences through your Netflix account settings or by following unsubscribe instructions included in communications.

Advertising Choices

Netflix provides options to manage behavioral advertising preferences and supports industry self-regulatory advertising programs that allow users to exercise additional privacy choices.

Cookie Choices

You can manage cookies and similar technologies through Netflix privacy settings and browser controls. These technologies help Netflix provide services, personalize experiences, and support advertising.

Section C: Access to Account and Profiles

Sharing Your Account

If you share your Netflix account with members of your household, they may have access to viewing activity, account information, profile information, and saved game data.

Profiles

Profiles provide personalized recommendations and separate viewing histories. Anyone with access to your account may access or modify profiles unless protected by a Profile Lock PIN.

Profile Transfers

Eligible profiles can be transferred to another Netflix account, including viewing history and recommendations.

Section D: Cookies and Other Technologies

Cookies and Similar Technologies

Netflix uses cookies, browser storage, pixel tags, hashed identifiers, and resettable device identifiers to provide services, improve performance, support advertising, and enhance security.

Why Netflix Uses These Technologies

These technologies help Netflix remember users, analyze service usage, measure marketing effectiveness, personalize content, support advertisements, and prevent fraud.

Section E: Other Important Privacy Disclosures

Security

Netflix uses administrative, technical, physical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

Retention of Personal Information

Personal information is retained only as long as necessary for business, legal, operational, and security purposes, after which it is securely deleted or de-identified.

Minors

Users must be at least 18 years old or the age of majority in their country to subscribe to Netflix. Minors may use the service only under parental supervision.

Changes to This Privacy Statement

Netflix may update this Privacy Statement periodically to reflect legal, regulatory, or operational changes. Continued use of the service indicates acceptance of those updates.

Section F: Netflix Games Supplemental Privacy Disclosures

Netflix Games may collect additional information such as game handles, gameplay activity, achievements, friend connections, chat content, user-generated content, and multiplayer interactions.
